About CafMetric

A simple answer to a critical question

Caffeine interferes with cardiac stress testing — but until now, there hasn't been a fast, definitive way to know if a patient's caffeine levels are safe to proceed. CafMetric changes that.

We're building a rapid point-of-care test that gives cardiologists a clear yes-or-no answer on blood caffeine concentration, eliminating guesswork, reducing rescheduled procedures, and improving the reliability of diagnostic results.

Binary Result

A clear yes or no — no interpretation needed. Safe to test, or reschedule with certainty.

Point-of-Care

Designed for use in the cardiology office, right before the procedure.

Clinical Confidence

Removes a common source of ambiguity in cardiac stress testing workflows.
